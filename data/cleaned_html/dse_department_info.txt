DESCRIPTION:
The Department of Data Science and Engineering (DSE) at IISER Bhopal focuses on training students and researchers in the fields of Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computational Methods. 
The department offers BS-MS, MS, and PhD programs with a strong emphasis on research and interdisciplinary applications.
